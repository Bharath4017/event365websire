The App is an events and happenings finder using the latest digital geolocation methods. The App will get you (hereafter referred to as “you”) one step closer to the hottest, most talked about events near you. By swiping left or right on the screen, you can view hundreds or even thousands of events near you using your phone’s GPS or similar locator. 365Live allows you to organise how you would like to party and be where it’s happening. A map icon allows for an easy overview of all of the events listed near your set location, or 365Live allows search for a specific event by entering the event code in the extended search menu. For travel purposes, you can even set your preferable distance of travel.
